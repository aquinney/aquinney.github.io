Target Audience

Who: Anybody who can read and navigate the internet.

Age: 8+

Income: Any amount.

Interests: People who are affected by weather conditions. This could be because of perceived road hazards while driving, outdoor activities, or any other reason one would need to know the weather. Perhaps it's just curiosity.

Personas

Jennifer

Occupation: Goes to elementary school

Reason for using site:Jennifer wants to ride bikes with her friends, but only if it's not raining.

Bill

Occupation: Retired.

Reason for using site:Bill's garden needs some attention, but he doesn't want to over water it. So he needs to know if it'll rain the weekend or not.

Scenarios

1. Is it going to be hot tomorrow?

2. Should I wear a sweater or a thick coat today?

3. Will me and my family be able to go camping this weekend?

4. Can I go for a jog, or do I need to use my indoor treadmill?

5. Will the roads be slick on my way into work this morning?
